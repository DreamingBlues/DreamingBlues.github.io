About Me

Hello, I'm Abtin Olaee. I'm currently pursuing a Master of Science degree in Data Science at San José State University. Previously, I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics and Data Science from the University of California, Santa Barbara.

My background spans data engineering, database management, and cloud platforms including AWS and GCP. I also have experience in mathematical modeling and predictive analytics, with a focus on building systems that support effective human-computer interaction.

My current interests lie in Applied Machine Learning in Real-World Systems and Quantitative Finance & Time-Series Modeling. Specifically, I’m focused on how innovative AI tools can be leveraged to bridge the gap between academic research and real-world impact.
